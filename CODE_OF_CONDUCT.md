# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the RealTimeToolkit.NET community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, gender identity, level of experience, or background.

## Our Standards
Positive behavior examples:
- Showing empathy and respect toward others
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Public or private harassment of any kind
- Publishing others’ private information

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to **Khujrat S Shaikh** at [your-email@example.com].  
Maintainers will review and take appropriate action as necessary.
